FAQ

  • >Q: Are teams allowed?
    Yes, only one member should submit. Include the names of all other team members in the form.
  • >Q: Is there a required length?
    Films should be at least one minute. There is a soft limit of five minutes, excluding credits and title sequences.
  • >Q: What is included in the 48 hour timeframe?
    Animation, filming, post-production (ie. editing, compositing), ideation/pre-production (ie. crew assembly, concept art) can be done beforehand.
  • >Q: Is there a specific medium?
    No. All mediums and styles are welcome, but be aware of the unique challenges of each medium and make sure to account for them. If you plan to do 3D animation, keep in mind that rendering is included in the 48 hour timeframe and, if you plan on working traditionally (raw film/tradition handdrawn animation) be conscious of lighting and resolution when digitising.
  • >Q: How good does my short have to be?
    Embrace the messiness and intuition, take as many shortcuts as you need, and, overall, have fun making the shorts.
